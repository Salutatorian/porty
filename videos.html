Browser audio tips

Chrome and Firefox decode video differently. If a clip has sound in one browser but not the other, the file usually needs AAC-LC audio in an H.264 MP4—re-encode and re-upload. The small button under each title forces unmute and full volume when the built-in speaker control acts stuck.
